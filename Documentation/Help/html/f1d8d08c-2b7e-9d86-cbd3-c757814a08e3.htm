SnapshotBase getLocalControlVariable Method Weverca documentation
Get snapshot entry for variable, used for extra info controlling in local context. Control entries may share names with other variables, indexes or fields. Control entries are not affected by unknown fields, also they cannot be aliased to non-control entries.
